# Incident And Serious Event Process

## Scope

This process covers incidents and serious events related to the Contextprime system.

## Trigger Conditions

Start this process when any of the following occurs:

1. Repeated unsafe or materially incorrect output that can affect users.
2. Confirmed security breach, unauthorized access, or model tampering.
3. Major service failure causing incorrect system behavior.
4. Any event that could create risk to health, safety, or fundamental rights.

## Immediate Actions

1. Open incident ticket with timestamp, owner, and affected services.
2. Preserve logs, request payloads, and system metrics.
3. Contain impact by disabling affected feature paths when needed.
4. Notify operations and product owners.

## Investigation Record

Record at minimum:

1. What happened.
2. Who was affected.
3. Root cause.
4. Corrective actions.
5. Preventive actions.
6. Validation evidence after mitigation.

## Reporting

For any serious incident in a regulated deployment context:

1. Escalate to legal and compliance owners immediately.
2. Follow applicable reporting timelines and authority notification rules.
3. Keep an immutable record of submitted reports and regulator correspondence.

## Closure Criteria

1. Corrective action deployed.
2. Monitoring confirms recovery.
3. Post incident review completed and signed.
4. Knowledge base updated.
